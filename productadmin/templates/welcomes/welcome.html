How does ShopMe work?

ShopMe offers a straightforward web application which allows you to view your booking schedule, add or cancel appointments for any service and offers a platform which your customers can use to purchase any type of product you have to offer.

What is a Product Page URL?

The Product Page URL refers to the url which ShopMe offers to your business which can be put in your Instagram bio's, embedded in your business websites and/or shared to your clients. This URL will take your clients to your product page where they can view all of the products that you have to offer and purchase them. These customers can modify the products to their specification and provide all the extra notes needed for their custom product.

This allows your clients to purchase custom made products directly from you without the back and forth conversation.
